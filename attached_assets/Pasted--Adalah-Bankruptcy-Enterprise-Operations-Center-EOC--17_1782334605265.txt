Enterprise Operations Center

Objective

Implement an Enterprise Operations Center for Bankruptcy Management.

This layer provides platform-wide monitoring, governance, storage management, backup monitoring, AI usage analytics, business intelligence, and emergency controls.

Must integrate with existing Bankruptcy V1, V2, V3 systems.

No breaking changes.

No duplicate business logic.
