EXECUTION DIRECTIVE — JUSTICE LEGAL WORLD MODEL (JLWM)

Objective

Implement a new enterprise-grade intelligence layer inside Adala called:

Justice Legal World Model (JLWM)

JLWM must transform Adala from a legal management platform into an intelligent legal operating system capable of:

* Building digital twins.
* Predicting legal outcomes.
* Monitoring legal risks.
* Generating recommendations.
* Simulating legal scenarios.
* Exploring future paths.
* Providing executive intelligence.

This implementation must be fully isolated, production-ready, multi-tenant aware, scalable, and must not break or modify existing functionality.

⸻

Critical Rules

1. Do not modify existing database tables.
2. Do not rename existing APIs.
3. Do not change existing business logic.
4. All new tables must use the prefix:

jlwm_

5. All routes must be namespaced under:

/api/jlwm/*

6. All data access must be tenant isolated using office_id.
7. Use existing authentication and authorization systems.
8. Reuse existing AI infrastructure.
9. Reuse existing audit logging.
10. Reuse existing office_ai_credits accounting.

⸻
