LEGAL AI PLATFORM – CONFLICT DETECTION & SYSTEM CONSISTENCY AUDIT

Objective

Perform a complete enterprise-grade audit of the entire Adalah AI platform to identify all logical conflicts, security conflicts, data inconsistencies, permission violations, UI inconsistencies, accounting discrepancies, performance bottlenecks, AI isolation issues, and multi-tenant architecture weaknesses.

The audit must simulate a production environment and assume thousands of active law firms.

⸻

1. Multi-Tenant Isolation Audit

Verify:

* All business tables contain office_id.
* All database queries enforce office-level filtering.
* No API endpoint can access another office’s data.
* No dashboard metrics aggregate data across offices unintentionally.
* No export, report, search, AI query, or analytics endpoint leaks external office data.
* No shared cache contamination between offices.
* No session leakage between tenants.

Required Output:

* Isolation Score (%)
* Critical Breaches
* Recommended Fixes
* Required Database Changes

3. Case Management Integrity Audit

Verify:

* Every case belongs to a valid client.
* Every hearing belongs to a valid case.

* Deleted clients cannot leave orphan records.

Detect all integrity conflicts.
